GameHub - The universe of games

Branding and webdesign for GameHub, an online game store that provides digital games to gamers at a competitive price. The gaming marketplace lets you buy tons of video games online for any device.

The target audience for the website are users between 18 to 25 who are interested in computer games, and would like to purchase games at a good price.

Community Science Museum

Branding and website designed for an interactive science museum called; Community Science Museum.

The target audience for the website are primary and middle school children (7-15) and families with young children.

The selling points for the website is; a website that is informative and appealing. It should also be engaging but encourage viewers to visit the museum itself.
